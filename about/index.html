About

Dr. Nicole Liliane MAFFO MAFFO MAFFO was born in Douala and completed all her primary and secondary education there. After obtaining the Baccalaureate series D, she lives in Yaoundé where she did all her university studies until she obtained her Doctorate/Ph.d in Plant Biology in 2018, with specialty in Ethnopharmacology/Plant Biodiversity/Environmental Ecotoxycology at the Department of Plant Biology of the University of Yaoundé I. Nicole has over 7 years of experience working in the area of Ethnopharmacology and Plant Biodiversity researches. She started her professional career in 2009 at the Ministry of Environment and Sustainable Development of Cameroon as a support framework within the framework of the PRECESSE Project. Since December 2011, Nicole has been recruited as a Teacher-Researcher at the Assistant level in the Department of Plant Biology of the University of Yaoundé I (Cameroon) and has been promoted to the rank of Lecturer since January 2019. Nicole Liliane has so far published a number of article in national and international peer review journals. She is member of the Higher Women Consortium since 2015. She has 01 child.

Dr. Nicole Liliane MAFFO MAFFO MAFFO was born in Douala and completed all her primary and secondary education there. After obtaining the Baccalaureate series D, she lives in Yaoundé where she did all her university studies until she obtained her Doctorate/Ph.d in Plant Biology in 2018, with specialty in Ethnopharmacology/Plant Biodiversity/Environmental Ecotoxycology at the Department of Plant Biology of the University of Yaoundé I. Nicole has over 7 years of experience working in the area of Ethnopharmacology and Plant Biodiversity researches.
